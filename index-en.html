What is Repair Café?

About Repair Café

Repair Café is a free community gathering where local volunteers help repair broken items — clothing, electronics, bicycles, furniture and more. Some locations also serve tea and snacks.

Repairing something together with someone makes us kinder. There is a story behind every worn-out object, and someone who will listen. In that shared moment, the item regains its life — and your attachment to it grows deeper.

3 Values

What Repair Café Stands For

👋

Accessibility

Drop-in visits are free. Everyone is welcome. Repair sessions may have a small fee or parts cost, but we aim to keep participation as open as possible.

🤝

Community

This is not a perfect-service commercial shop. It is a space to learn together through repair, and to connect with people and your neighbourhood.

🌱

Sustainability

Through repair, we extend the life of objects. We spread a mindset and practice that cares for people, communities, and the environment.

Who We Are

Repair Café Japan is a program operated by Repair Alliance, a Japanese non-profit organization, supporting the growth and development of Repair Cafés across Japan.

Using things with care for a long time, while feeling connected to community — this is also an invitation to rethink what it means to live a rich life.
